Boiled Eggs

Description

This method for hard boiling eggs is simple and produces a tender and delicious egg every time! Cooking times vary depending on the type of cooktop (electric, gas, and induction) and size of egg, among other factors. Play around with the timing based on your hard boiled egg preferences.

Ingredients

  • water
  • eggs

Steps

  1. Place cold eggs in a single layer in a saucepan. Cover with at least 1 inch (2.5 cm) cold water over top of the eggs.
  2. Cover saucepan and bring quickly to a boil over a high heat. Immediately remove pan from heat to stop boiling. Let eggs stand in water for 12 minutes (large eggs).
